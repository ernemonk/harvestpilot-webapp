## Executive Vision

HarvestPilot is reimagining microgreens farming as **plug-and-play automation that just works**. We strip away complexity, focus on what farmers actually need, and deliver reliable systems that grow better crops with less effort.

**Farmer-First Philosophy:**
- **Simplicity over features** — One-button setup, intuitive controls
- **Reliability over innovation** — Systems that work every day, not just demos
- **Results over complexity** — Better yields, easier harvesting, happier farmers
- **Community over corporation** — Open designs, farmer-driven improvements

## The Problem (Farmer Perspective)

Farmers tell us they struggle with:
- **Inconsistent results** — Same seeds, different yields
- **Time wasted on manual tasks** — Watering, checking, adjusting
- **Lost crops from mistakes** — Over/under watering, missed schedules
- **No visibility** — Can't monitor when away from the farm
- **Expensive equipment** — $2,000+ systems that break or don't scale

**Our mission: Make microgreens farming as simple as growing herbs in a windowsill.**
